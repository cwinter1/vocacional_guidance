# מדריך מקצועי — Career Guidance

An interactive Hebrew questionnaire that helps bankers identify the best career transition path into hi-tech, consulting, or accounting.

## What it does

- **Glossary** — 12 hi-tech terms explained with banking analogies
- **12 role cards** — each with a real day-in-the-life breakdown, hours rating, and office-presence rating
- **25 questions** — all scenarios rooted in banking context, no hi-tech assumed
- **Scored results** — top 3 role matches, next steps, hours/office comparison table, and a direct answer to "should I study Accounting 1-2-3?"

## Roles covered

PM · Systems Analyst · Business Analyst · ERP Consultant · Implementation Manager · Customer Success · RegTech · Data Analyst · QA · Scrum Master · Life Coach · CPA Track

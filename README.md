# מדריך מקצועי — Career Guidance

An interactive Hebrew questionnaire that helps bankers identify the best career transition path into hi-tech, consulting, compliance, finance, or education roles.

## What it does

- **Glossary** — 12 hi-tech terms explained with banking analogies
- **20 role cards** — each with a real day-in-the-life breakdown, hours rating, office-presence rating, and B2B/B2C label
- **30 questions** — 25 scenario-based (sections א–ה) + 5 bridge/aspirational questions (section ו)
- **5 career domain bars** — ניהול ותיאום · ניתוח ומחקר · פיננסים ורגולציה · אנשים ולקוחות · מערכות ותפעול
- **Scored results** — top 3 role matches, personalized insight cards, hours/office comparison table, and an adaptive answer to "should I study for CPA?"

## Roles covered

**Hi-Tech / Operations**
PM · Systems Analyst · Business Analyst · ERP Consultant · Implementation Manager · Customer Success · RegTech · Data Analyst · QA · Scrum Master

**Finance / Compliance**
CPA Track · Compliance Manager · Investment Advisor · Mortgage Advisor

**People / Education**
Life Coach · L&D Manager · Financial Literacy Educator · Customer Experience Manager

**Content**
Financial Content Writer
